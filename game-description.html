The Four Pillars of Empire

Every decision shapes your civilization. Explore unknown frontiers, expand your borders, exploit the land, and exterminate those who stand in your way.

Explore

Chart unknown lands, dispatch scouts, and reveal resources from wheat fields to iron mines.

Expand

Found cities across continents, claim territory, and build roads that bind your empire together.

Exploit

Assign citizens, improve tiles, trade luxuries, and balance food, production, gold, and science.

Exterminate

Wage tactical wars with unique units, promotions, and combined-arms combat across land and sea.

Choose Your Legacy

From Sumerian war-carts to Mongol hordes and Inca terrace farms, each of the 130+ civilizations brings a unique leader, ability, unit, and infrastructure spanning every region of the ancient and medieval world.

A Living World of Hexes

Procedural maps and real-world geodata presets mean no two games are alike. Deserts, jungles, mountains, rivers, and coastlines shape every empire's story.

  • Procedural continents, archipelagos, and Earth presets
  • Bonus, luxury, and strategic resources to fight over
  • Combined-arms tactics with melee, ranged, cavalry, and siege
  • Districts, wonders, and veteran units with earned names
