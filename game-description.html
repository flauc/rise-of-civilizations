Rise of Civilizations - Forge an Empire Through the Ages

4000 BCE - 1550 CE

Rise of Civilizations is a turn-based 4X strategy game played in your browser. Lead one of 130+ civilizations from the first mud-brick cities to the Age of Exploration, across 5 eras and 85+ technologies.

The Four Pillars of Empire

Every decision shapes your civilization. Explore unknown frontiers, expand your borders, exploit the land, and exterminate those who stand in your way.

  • Explore - Chart unknown lands, dispatch scouts, and reveal resources from wheat fields to iron mines.
  • Expand - Found cities across continents, claim territory, and build roads that bind your empire together.
  • Exploit - Assign citizens, improve tiles, trade luxuries, and balance food, production, gold, and science.
  • Exterminate - Wage tactical wars with unique units, promotions, and combined-arms combat across land and sea.

Choose Your Legacy

From Sumerian war-carts to Mongol hordes and Inca terrace farms, each of the 130+ civilizations brings a unique leader, ability, unit, and infrastructure spanning every region of the ancient and medieval world.

Five Ages of History

Advance from stone tools to square-rigged galleons. Each era unlocks new units, buildings, governments, and ways to win.

Era Years Highlights
Stone / Dawn 4000–3000 BCE First tribes, farms, and mud-brick cities.
Bronze Age 3000–1200 BCE Chariots, ziggurats, and the first empires.
Iron / Classical 1200 BCE–500 CE Legions, phalanxes, philosophy, and great roads.
Medieval / Faith 500–1300 CE Knights, castles, samurai, and cathedrals.
Exploration 1300–1550 CE Caravels, gunpowder, printing, and global trade.

A Living World of Hexes

Procedural maps and real-world geodata presets mean no two games are alike. Deserts, jungles, mountains, rivers, and coastlines shape every empire's story.

  • Procedural continents, archipelagos, and Earth presets
  • Bonus, luxury, and strategic resources to fight over
  • Combined-arms tactics with melee, ranged, cavalry, and siege
  • Districts, wonders, and veteran units with earned names

The World Awaits Its Next Ruler

Play single-player against AI, hotseat with friends, or join online multiplayer. Your empire begins with a single settler. No install required - works on desktop and mobile.
