# Promotions

Promotions are earned when a unit gains enough experience (XP) from combat, clearing barbarian camps, or discovering villages. Each level-up grants one unspent promotion that can be spent immediately or saved. Promotions are class-specific: a unit can only choose from the pool matching its unit class.

## Core Rules

- Units gain **1 XP per combat** they survive, plus bonus XP for kills.
- Every **10 XP per current level** is required to level up.
- Promotions are permanent and stack.
- Promotions are **tiered**. A unit unlocks tier 1 at level 2, tier 2 at level 3, and tier 3 at level 4.
- Some promotions apply only on attack, only on defense, or only in specific terrain.

## Design Notes

- **Melee** promotions emphasize raw combat, city assault, and defensive lines.
- **Cavalry** promotions reward speed, flanking, and hit-and-run tactics.
- **Ranged** promotions focus on range, positioning, and anti-unit fire.
- **Siege** promotions specialize in breaking cities and static defenses.
- **Recon** promotions maximize mobility, vision, and ambush potential.
- **Civilian** promotions are rare (earned from villages or special effects) and help settlers/workers survive and move faster.
